Data Notes & Methodology
Transparency on data sources, cleaning decisions and DAX measures used

Data Sources

This dashboard was built from 5 raw exports from Goomti's Flipdish POS and delivery platform:

📊 Dine_Items_sales.xlsx 📋 June_Dine_in_sales_per_day.csv 📁 dashboard-business_review.zip 📁 dashboard-menu_items_report.zip 📁 dashboard-sales_summary_report.zip

The data covers 25 May 2026 to 23 June 2026 (approximately 30 days). Historical order data (Jan–Mar 2026) was extracted from the business review ZIP for the customer trend chart.

Outlier Identification & Removal

During data preparation, a "COLD TAKEAWAY FOOD" category was identified as an outlier group:

  • 1,103 units sold — highest volume category by a significant margin
  • £0.00 net revenue — zero monetary contribution despite high unit count
  • Items include: Cold Pakora/Bhaji Trays, Cold Samosas, Cold Sandwiches, Cold Wraps — all pre-packaged zero-price items

Decision: Excluded from all menu performance analysis. They remain in raw source data and are documented here for transparency. Inclusion would have distorted revenue-per-item calculations and category rankings.

Method: String-match filter on item names containing "Cold", "Chilled", or "Takeaway" combined with a £0 revenue filter.

Also removed: A grand total row exported at the bottom of fact_sales_overview.csv which contained the period total (£18,859, 418 orders) as a data row — this was causing all KPI measures to double-count.

DAX Measures Used

-- Core revenue measures Total Net Sales = SUM(fact_sales_overview[Net_Sales]) Total Orders = SUM(fact_sales_overview[Orders]) Avg Order Value = DIVIDE([Total Net Sales], [Total Orders], 0) -- Weekend analysis using CALCULATE + IN filter Weekend Revenue = CALCULATE([Total Net Sales], fact_sales_overview[Day_of_Week] IN {"Friday","Saturday","Sunday"}) Weekend Pct = DIVIDE([Weekend Revenue], [Total Net Sales], 0) -- Menu measures Dine-in Menu Sales = SUM(dim_menu_items[Net_Sales]) Total Items Sold = SUM(dim_menu_items[Items_Sold]) -- Channel measures Dine-in Sales = CALCULATE(SUM(dim_channels[Net_Sales]), dim_channels[Channel] = "POS / Dine-in") Delivery Sales = CALCULATE(SUM(dim_channels[Net_Sales]), dim_channels[Type] = "Delivery") -- Customer metrics (hardcoded — source data is summary level, not row level) Unique Customers = 124 Repeat Customers = 16 Repeat Customer Pct = 0.13 Retention Opportunity = ROUND(124 * 0.87 * 0.10 * 45.12, 0)

Known Data Limitations

  • Tuesday revenue = £0 on all weeks — confirmed closed day, not missing data
  • 10pm hourly spike (£8,135) likely reflects delivery batch processing timestamps, not actual 10pm dine-in covers
  • Customer data covers online/delivery channel only — walk-in dine-in customers without accounts are not captured, so real unique customer count is higher than 124
  • COGS shows £0 across all records — not populated in source system, so Gross Profit = Net Sales throughout
  • Customer hardcoded measures (Unique Customers = 124 etc.) are sourced from summary-level business review export — a recommendation for the business is to enable Customer ID tracking across all channels for proper RFM analysis
